Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum monthly ad spend required?
To ensure we have enough data to optimize and scale your campaigns effectively, we recommend a minimum monthly ad budget of $2,000 for local/service businesses and $5,000 for e-commerce.
Do you lock clients into long-term contracts?
No. We believe in earning your business every single month. After an initial 90-day sprint (required for algorithm learning and pixel optimization), our contracts shift to a flexible month-to-month model.
How long does it take to see an ROI?
Our paid acquisition campaigns (Google & Meta Ads) typically start generating a positive ROAS within the first 30 to 45 days. The first month is heavily focused on data gathering, tracking setup, and aggressive A/B testing.
Who will be managing my account?
Unlike traditional agencies that pass you off to junior account managers, you will work directly with the founders, Noman Ahmad and Rayhan Biswas. You get direct Slack/WhatsApp access to the experts actually running your campaigns.
